Within the realm of computer science, there are several possible specific major focuses. You may choose not to have a focus and study general computer science, or you may have a preference for a specific line of work. If you are interested in video games, you may choose a more art-centric gaming focus. If you are interested in or concerned about hacking, you may opt for a cybersecurity major that can teach you both how to hack and how to create secure programs. If you are interested in starting your own software company or rising to a high level in another large company, you might find a computer science with business degree alluring.

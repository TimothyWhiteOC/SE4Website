Business:
What is
Computer Science
With Business?



What is it?

 Computer Science with Business is a degree that combines all
 of the theoretical basics of Computer Science while including
 a the basic foundations of business. This allows for a technical
 degree with strong foundations in interpersonal business skills.



Job Opportunities:

 Computer Science with business equips alumni to excel in large
 firms, start-up companies, and can ultimately lead to a senior
 managment position. A popular position that encompases both
 focuses is called a Scrum Master

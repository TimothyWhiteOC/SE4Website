Studying Computers:
What is it
like?

What you will do:

 Since computer science deals with theoretical foundations of
 information, you will be doing things like learning different
 programming languages, studying algorithms, using logic to
 solve issues, and working in teams.



Is it hard?

 One of the most common questions from highschool students is
 "Is Computer Science Hard?" Unfortunatley, this major has the highest
 drop out rate out of all majors. If you are logically inclined and
 enjoy a bit of a challenge, computer science might be for you


















What is the job like?

 Most computer scientists work in teams in flexible environments
 86% of IT developers worked remotely in 2019
 Computer Science related jobs score high on work/life balance
 parameters.



Available Majors:

 There are many variations in what major you
 can study within the field of computer science.
 Some of these range from cybersecurity to gaming.
